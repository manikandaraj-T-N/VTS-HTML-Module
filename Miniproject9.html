Healthy Fruit Habits

Published on: May 7, 2025 | Author: Manikandaraj

Why Fruits Are Important

Fruits are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. Including a variety of fruits in your diet helps maintain overall health.

Top 5 Fruits to Include in Your Diet

  1. Bananas – Great source of potassium
  2. Apples – Rich in fiber and antioxidants
  3. Blueberries – Packed with antioxidants
  4. Oranges – Loaded with vitamin C
  5. Mangoes – Delicious and full of nutrients

Quick Tips to Eat More Fruits

  • Keep cut fruits in your fridge for easy snacking
  • Add fruits to your breakfast cereal or smoothie
  • Use fruits as a natural sweetener in desserts
